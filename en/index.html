About Us

Stop by "Za zakrętem", a campsite in Barycz Valley created with cyclists in mind!

We run an intimate campsite located along the Lower Silesian Cycling Region routes and the EuroVelo 9 trail. We offer peaceful accommodation near Milicz Ponds, forests and Natura 2000 areas. This place was created by lovers of tourism and cycling sports and tested by tourists with panniers, gravel and MTB riders.

We offer:

  • Accommodation in camping trailers
  • Campsite
  • Motorhome pitch
  • Bike and helmet rental
  • Motorhome service
  • Bike repairs
  • Purchase of bike parts on site

Facilities:

  • Shower and toilet
  • Access to electricity
  • Access to basic bike repair tools
  • Lockable bike parking
  • Fenced property
  • Parking on site
  • Nearby shop, bar, and food truck (ice cream, waffles, coffee)
  • Location right on the Lower Silesian Cycling Region routes and EuroVelo 9 trail
  • Proximity to ponds, forests and "Natura 2000" areas
We speak English and German!
